[ 01 / 04 ] About

A designer with a research-led approach.

I'm Diego Alejandro Gómez Copete, a Graphic Designer from Universidad Los Libertadores, based in Bogotá. I help small businesses and agencies turn complex briefs into clean, confident visual systems — with a soft spot for the web.

Background. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design from Universidad Los Libertadores. Across the last several years I've focused on web design, UI, and front-end implementation — designing interfaces and shipping them in code.

How I work. Every project starts with research and conceptualization: understanding the audience, the message, and the constraints before a single pixel moves. From there I build strategic, optimal solutions in visual communication that hold up across screens and surfaces.

What I love. The intersection of design and code — moments where a thoughtful layout, a well-paired type system, and a few lines of HTML and CSS quietly do real work for a business.

Always learning. Picking up new tools, frameworks, and techniques — staying close to current front-end practice and to design tools that make the work better.
